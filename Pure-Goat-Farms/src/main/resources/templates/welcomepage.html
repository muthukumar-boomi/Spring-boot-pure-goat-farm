Saanen

Saanen goats are known for outstanding milk production

The Saanen goat originates from the Saanen Valley in Switzerland. They are the largest of the Swiss breeds and are often called the "Holstein of the dairy goat world" due to their high milk production.

Saanens are docile, calm, and adaptable to various climates, making them popular worldwide for commercial dairy.

History of the Saanen

The Saanen breed was developed in the Saanen Valley of Switzerland and has been exported worldwide for its exceptional milk production.
